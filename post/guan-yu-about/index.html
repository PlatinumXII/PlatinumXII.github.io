我 Me

我是顾博仁，目前就读于爱丁堡大学数学物理专业。

场论、低维拓扑学、凝聚态理论以及数学物理和理论物理的许多其他方面都是我感兴趣的方向，其中纽结理论尤其令人着迷。

我的简历可能有助于您更好地了解我。

I am Boren, currently an undergraduate student majoring Mathematical Physics at the University of Edinburgh, Scotland.

I am interested in the fields of field theory, low dimensional topology, condensed matter theory and many other aspects of mathematical physics and theoretical physics.

Especially, if you have the interest in knot theory, we may build a friendship from it.

For more information, please check my résumé.
